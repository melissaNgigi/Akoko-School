Past Principals

Leaders Who Shaped Our Legacy

Since our establishment in 1965, St. Paul's Akoko Secondary School has been led by distinguished educators who have contributed significantly to our growth and development. Each principal has left an indelible mark on our institution's history.

Mr. James Okello

1965 - 1975

Founding Principal who established the school's core values and initial infrastructure.

Mrs. Grace Wambui

1975 - 1985

Expanded academic programs and introduced new sports facilities.

Dr. Peter Ouma

1985 - 1995

Modernized teaching methods and established computer laboratories.

Mrs. Elizabeth Maobe

1995 - 2010

Led the school through significant academic improvements and infrastructure development.

Mr. David Onyango

2010 - 2020

Enhanced community engagement and established international partnerships.
