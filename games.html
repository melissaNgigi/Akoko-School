About Our Department

The Games Department promotes physical education and sports activities. We organize and coordinate all sports activities and competitions in the school.

Sports Activities:

  • Team Sports: Football, Volleyball, Handball
  • Individual Sports: Athletics, Table Tennis
  • Competitions: Inter-house, Inter-school, and County levels
  • Training: Regular coaching and practice sessions
