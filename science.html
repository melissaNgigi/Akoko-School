About the Department

The Science Department at St. Paul's Akoko Secondary School is dedicated to fostering scientific inquiry and practical skills through hands-on experimentation and research. Our well-equipped laboratories provide students with the perfect environment for learning Biology, Chemistry, and Physics.

Our department has consistently produced outstanding results in KCSE examinations, with many of our students pursuing careers in medicine, engineering, and other science-related fields. In 2023, we achieved remarkable success across all science subjects.
