About the Department

The Mathematics Department at St. Paul's Akoko Secondary School has been a cornerstone of academic excellence since the school's establishment. Our department takes pride in nurturing mathematical thinking and problem-solving skills essential for the 21st century.

Over the years, we have maintained exceptional performance in KCSE, with our students consistently achieving above-average results. In 2023, the department recorded a mean grade of 8.2, with 18 students scoring A grades.
