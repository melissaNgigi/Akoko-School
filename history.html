Our History

A Legacy of Excellence Since Establishment

Our Journey Through Time

St. Paul's Akoko Mixed Secondary School is a seven streamed county public institution located in Akoko village, Central Sakwa location Bondo Sub-county in Siaya County. The school was established in the year 1986 with the first Principal of the school being Mr. Nestor C Ndolo

Since then, the school has grown in terms of the quality and quantity of the infrastructure. The main financial contributors to the growth of the facilities are CDF, parents and MOE. The school has also grown in terms of student enrolment. There were 25 students in 1986 when the school was founded. The current student population stands at 1294.

On religious affiliation, the School is sponsored by Anglican Church of Kenya
