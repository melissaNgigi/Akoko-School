Co-curricular Activities

Sports

At St. Paul's Akoko Secondary School, we believe in nurturing both mind and body. Our sports program includes football, volleyball, basketball, athletics, and rugby. Our teams have consistently performed well in county and regional competitions.

Our dedicated coaches and modern sports facilities enable students to develop their athletic abilities while learning valuable lessons in teamwork, discipline, and leadership. We've produced several national-level athletes and continue to excel in various sporting events.

Clubs & Societies

Our diverse range of clubs and societies caters to various student interests and talents. From Science Club to Debate Society, Environmental Club to Young Farmers Club, we offer numerous opportunities for students to explore their passions.

These clubs organize regular activities, competitions, and field trips, helping students develop leadership skills, creativity, and practical knowledge outside the classroom. Many of our clubs have won recognition in national competitions.

Performing Arts & Music

Our performing arts and music program is a vibrant part of school life. Students participate in drama, music festivals, choir, and traditional dance performances. The school has a well-equipped music room and dedicated performance spaces.

Our students regularly participate in county and national music and drama festivals, often bringing home top honors. These activities help develop confidence, creativity, and appreciation for cultural arts.
