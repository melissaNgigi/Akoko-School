KCSE Performance

A Track Record of Academic Excellence

St. Paul's Akoko Secondary School has consistently maintained high academic standards, as evidenced by our KCSE results over the years. Since 2017, we have seen a steady improvement in our mean grade from 6.8 to our current 7.8, demonstrating our commitment to academic excellence. This progress is reflected in the growing number of students achieving top grades and qualifying for university admission, with our university qualification rate rising from 65% in 2017 to 85% in 2023.
