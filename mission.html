Our core business

As a registered public school in the Republic of Kenya, we discharge our duties in accordance with the regulations set out to us by the Ministry of Education and its agencies, as well as the laws of the land.

Generally, therefore, our mandate encompasses the following:

  1. Implementing the teaching/ learning curriculum as designed by the KICD, MOE and TSC
  2. Inculcating acceptable values that would enable the students to develop a sense of nationhood, be productive in life, and fit within the fast- changing global society.
  3. Providing the opportunities for evocation of talents through co-curricular activities and clubs
  4. Enforcing a corporate social feeling
  5. Providing formative and continuous evaluation to the learners as they prepare for the summative evaluation that KNEC provides
  6. Sensitizing the learners to the rigorous world of tertiary studies and work
  7. Instilling a sense of discipline in the students
  8. Participating in community programmes
  9. Nurturing the religious inclinations of the learners
