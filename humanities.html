About the Department

The Humanities Department at St. Paul's Akoko Secondary School is dedicated to developing students' understanding of human society, culture, and relationships. Our programs in History, Geography, and Religious Education foster critical thinking and cultural awareness.

Through field trips, interactive learning, and engaging projects, we help students understand the world around them and their role in shaping its future. Our department has maintained excellent performance in KCSE examinations and continues to inspire future leaders in various fields.
