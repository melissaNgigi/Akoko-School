About Our Department

Welcome to the Humanities Department at St. Paul's Akoko Secondary School. Our department focuses on developing students' understanding of human society, history, geography, and business studies.

Subjects Offered:

  • History: Study of past events and their impact on society
  • Geography: Study of physical and human environments
  • CRE Study of religious beliefs and practices
