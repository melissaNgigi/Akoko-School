About the Department

The Languages Department at St. Paul's Akoko Secondary School is committed to developing students' communication skills in both English and Kiswahili. Our department emphasizes practical language use through debates, drama, and creative writing activities.

We have established a strong tradition of excellence in language arts, with our students regularly participating in national drama festivals and writing competitions. Our KCSE results demonstrate consistent improvement in both English and Kiswahili.
