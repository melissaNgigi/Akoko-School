About the Department

The Technical Department at St. Paul's Akoko Secondary School focuses on developing practical skills and technological knowledge. Our department offers courses in Computer Studies, Agriculture, and Business Studies, preparing students for both higher education and practical career paths.

With our modern computer laboratory and agricultural demonstration farm, we provide hands-on learning experiences that complement theoretical knowledge. Our students consistently perform well in KCSE examinations and various technical competitions.
